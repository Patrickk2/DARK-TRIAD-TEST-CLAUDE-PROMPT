DARK TRIAD PERSONALITY ASSESSMENT — ADMIN PROMPT
=================================================

Administer a high-accuracy Dark Triad personality assessment using your interactive quiz tool with button-based multiple choice inputs.

================================

Present ALL 27 items MIXED together (do not group by trait).

PHASE 2 — Behavioral Frequency (6 items)
------------------------------------------
These measure what you've ACTUALLY DONE, not attitudes.
Present all 6 at once. Each: single_select buttons → Never | Rarely | Sometimes | Often | Always

[B1] In the past year, how often did you deliberately withhold information from someone to gain an advantage?
[B2] When you've hurt someone emotionally, how quickly do you feel guilt? (Never | Days later | Hours later | Quickly | Immediately) ← reversed scale
[B3] How often do you mentally catalogue someone's weaknesses or insecurities?
[B4] How often do you bend or break rules when you're confident you won't get caught?
[B5] In conflicts, how often do you think about what you could leverage against the other person?
[B6] How often have you befriended someone primarily because of what they could do for you?

Scoring key (internal, don't show):
B1 → Psychopathy/Machiavellianism indicator
B2 → Psychopathy indicator (inverted: Never guilt = high P)
B3 → Machiavellianism indicator
B4 → Psychopathy indicator
B5 → Machiavellianism indicator
B6 → Machiavellianism/Narcissism indicator

Map: Never=1, Rarely=2, Sometimes=3, Often=4, Always=5
B2 inverted: Immediately=1, Quickly=2, Hours later=3, Days later=4, Never=5

---

PHASE 3 — Social Desirability Check (4 items)
-----------------------------------------------
These detect whitewashing. Present as one block.
Each: single_select buttons → 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5

[SD1] I have never taken advantage of someone, even when I could have.
[SD2] I always admit when I'm wrong, regardless of consequences.
[SD3] I have never said something unkind about someone behind their back.
[SD4] I follow rules even when no one is watching and there are no consequences.

---

SCORING INSTRUCTIONS (internal — never show raw calculation)
=============================================================

SD3 scores:
- Reverse items: score = 6 − answer
- Machiavellianism = avg(M1–M9)
- Narcissism = avg(N1–N9)
- Psychopathy = avg(P1–P9)
- Range: 1.0–5.0

Behavioral adjustment:
- Compute B_mach = avg(B1, B3, B5, B6), B_psych = avg(B2, B4)
- If B_mach differs from SD3 Mach score by more than 0.8 → flag inconsistency
- If B_psych differs from SD3 Psychopathy score by more than 0.8 → flag inconsistency
- Final adjusted score = (SD3_score × 0.65) + (behavioral_score × 0.35)

Social desirability (SD) check:
- SD_avg = avg(SD1–SD4)
- If SD_avg ≥ 4.0: scores are likely DEFLATED — add a "⚠ Whitewash Warning" to results
- If SD_avg ≤ 2.0: unusually self-critical — scores may be INFLATED — note this too

Population norms (undergraduate sample, Jones & Paulhus 2014):
- Machiavellianism: Mean = 3.0, SD = 0.7
- Narcissism: Mean = 2.9, SD = 0.7
- Psychopathy: Mean = 2.1, SD = 0.7
- Percentile = use z-score: z = (score − mean) / SD
  - z < −1.0 → bottom 16%
  - z −1.0 to −0.5 → below average
  - z −0.5 to +0.5 → average range
  - z +0.5 to +1.0 → above average
  - z > +1.0 → top 16%
  - z > +2.0 → top 2.5%
